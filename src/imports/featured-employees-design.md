Redesign the current **Top Rated Employees** section and transform it into a **Featured Employees** showcase that feels premium, engaging, and visually attractive.

Goal: Highlight a curated group of outstanding freelancers to encourage users to explore profiles and connect with them.

Design Requirements:

1. Section Identity

* Change the title to **“Featured Employees”**
* Add a short subtitle such as:
  *“Meet some of the most talented professionals ready to bring your ideas to life.”*
* The section should visually stand out from other page sections with a subtle gradient background or soft glow.

2. Card Design Improvements
   Each employee should appear in a modern **featured profile card** with:

* Large circular avatar with a soft glow or gradient ring
* Verified badge
* Employee name (bold)
* Role / specialization
* Location
* Rating with star icon
* Skill tags (React, Node.js, Figma, etc.)
* Hourly rate clearly emphasized

3. Featured Visual Hierarchy

* The **first employee card can be slightly larger** to indicate “Top Featured”.
* Use soft shadows and elevated card design.
* Add a subtle hover interaction that:

  * lifts the card
  * slightly scales it
  * reveals additional micro details (like years of experience or completed projects).

4. Animations (Subtle and Professional)
   Use smooth and lightweight animations:

* Cards should **fade in + slide up** when the section enters the viewport.
* Hover animation:

  * card lifts
  * shadow increases
  * connect button glows slightly
* Skill tags can have a small hover highlight.
* Buttons should have a soft gradient hover animation.

5. Layout

* Responsive grid layout
* Desktop: 3–4 cards per row
* Tablet: 2 cards
* Mobile: horizontal swipe carousel

6. Call to Action
   Buttons:

* **View Profile** (outline style)
* **Connect** (primary gradient button)

Add a **“View All Talent →”** link at the top right of the section.

7. Style Direction
   Modern, minimal, and elegant:

* Soft gradients
* Rounded cards (16px–20px radius)
* Smooth shadows
* Subtle glassmorphism or frosted card effect

8. Optional Enhancements

* Add a **“Featured” ribbon or badge**
* Show **number of completed projects**
* Add a **small animated sparkle or glow effect** around the featured badge.

Design should feel similar to modern platforms like **Dribbble, Upwork, or Stripe talent showcases** — clean, premium, and interactive.
